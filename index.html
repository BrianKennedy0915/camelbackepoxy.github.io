Why Choose Us

The Camelback Difference

Most epoxy companies apply coatings over improperly prepped concrete. We don't. Diamond grinding is non-negotiable on every job — it's the difference between a floor that lasts 15 years and one that peels in 18 months.

◆

Diamond-Grind Surface Prep — We grind every floor, every time. No acid wash shortcuts. This is why our floors bond stronger and last longer.

◆

100% Solids Epoxy Systems — Premium, professional-grade materials with zero solvents. Higher build, better resistance, longer life.

◆

Moisture Testing on Every Job — Arizona concrete can fail an epoxy system without proper moisture assessment. We test before we coat.

◆

5-Year Written Warranty — We back our work because we're confident in our process. No fine print, no exceptions.

◆

Same-Week Scheduling — We respect your time. Most residential jobs are scheduled within 5–7 business days and completed in one.

Our Process

1

Free On-Site Estimate

We come to you, assess your floor, perform moisture testing, and deliver a written quote — same day.

2

Diamond Grinding & Prep

We grind the entire surface, repair cracks, and clean edges. The prep determines the result.

3

Epoxy System Installation

Primer, base coat, decorative broadcast, and polyaspartic topcoat — all in one day for most residential jobs.

4

Cure, Walk-Through & Warranty
