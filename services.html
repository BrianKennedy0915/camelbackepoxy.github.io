Concrete Polishing

Polished Concrete

Polished concrete is the low-maintenance, high-impact alternative to traditional flooring. Ground progressively to a smooth, mirror-like finish, then sealed with a concrete densifier — it's one of the most durable and sophisticated floor surfaces available.

◆

No coatings to peel or chip — the concrete itself is the floor

◆

Multiple sheen levels — matte, satin, or high-gloss

◆

Aggregate exposure options — cream, salt-and-pepper, or full aggregate

◆

Ideal for retail, offices, modern homes
