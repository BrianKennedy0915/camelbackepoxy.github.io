Our Story

Built to Last

Camelback Epoxy Co. was founded on one belief: Scottsdale deserves a flooring company that does the job right — not just fast and cheap.

The Founding Story

Why Camelback Epoxy Exists

Camelback Epoxy Co. was founded by Brian — an entrepreneur with a background in digital marketing who saw firsthand how underserved the Scottsdale market was for quality epoxy flooring.

The problem wasn't a lack of contractors. It was a lack of contractors who cared enough to do the prep work right, use professional-grade materials, and actually warranty their results. Most operators acid-wash instead of diamond-grind, use low-grade materials, and disappear when problems arise.

Camelback Epoxy Co. was built to be different — a brand that Scottsdale homeowners and business owners can trust, built on process, quality, and accountability.

"I got into this business because I was tired of seeing homeowners get burned by bad prep work. Diamond grinding is the foundation of everything we do. If you skip it, you're not installing epoxy — you're just painting concrete."

— Brian, Founder & CEO

Our Standards

What We Never Compromise

◆

Diamond-Grind Every Time

We own professional-grade diamond grinding equipment and use it on every single job. Acid washing is a shortcut we will never take. Period. The grind is what makes epoxy bond permanently to concrete.

🔬

Moisture Test First

Arizona concrete contains moisture — especially in summer. We test every floor before applying anything. If moisture is elevated, we use a moisture-barrier primer that prevents failure. No guessing.

🏆

Professional-Grade Materials Only

We use 100% solids epoxy systems — no water-based, no solvent-cut products. Our polyaspartic topcoats are UV-stable and won't yellow in the Arizona sun. We use the same materials the pros use.

📋

Written Warranty, Always

Every completed job includes a signed 5-year workmanship warranty. If it peels, chips, or delaminated due to our installation, we fix it — no charge. We're confident enough in our work to put it in writing.

📱

We Answer the Phone

You should never chase down a contractor. We respond to every call and message within 2 hours during business hours. Estimates are delivered same-day. Scheduling is confirmed immediately.

🧹

Job Site Left Cleaner Than We Found It

Every job ends with a thorough cleanup. We protect your belongings, contain dust with HEPA vacuums during grinding, and leave your property spotless. Your garage, your business — we respect it.
