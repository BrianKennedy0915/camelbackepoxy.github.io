Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Camelback Epoxy Co. (operated by WKG LLC) is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when visiting camelbackepoxy.com or contacting us for services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address submitted via our estimate request form
  • Property address for scheduling purposes
  • Project details and preferences you share with us
  • Communications via phone, email, or text message

We may also automatically collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, browser type, device type) via tools such as Google Analytics. This data is anonymized and aggregated.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your estimate requests and schedule on-site visits
  • Provide quotes, project updates, and service communications
  • Follow up on completed projects for warranty and satisfaction purposes
  • Improve our website and services through analytics

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a secure third-party form service. Your data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We retain customer records as required for warranty and business purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and basic site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to use our site or submit estimate requests.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you at any time. To make such a request, contact us at hello@camelbackepoxy.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
